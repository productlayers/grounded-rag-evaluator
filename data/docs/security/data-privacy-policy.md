# Data Privacy Policy

This policy outlines how we collect, store, and protect user data across all services.

## Data Collection

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide the service. This includes:

- **Account information:** Name, email address, and hashed password.
- **Usage telemetry:** Anonymized feature usage statistics for product improvement.
- **Support interactions:** Transcripts of support conversations for quality assurance.

We do not sell personal data to third parties under any circumstances.

## Data Storage and Retention

All user data is stored in encrypted databases within SOC 2 Type II certified data centers located in the United States and European Union. Data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 and in transit using TLS 1.3.

Retention periods vary by data type:

- Account information is retained for the duration of the account plus 30 days after deletion.
- Usage telemetry is aggregated and anonymized after 90 days. Raw telemetry is purged after 90 days.
- Support transcripts are retained for 1 year for training purposes, then permanently deleted.

## User Rights

Users may exercise the following rights at any time by contacting support@example.com:

1. **Right to Access:** Request a copy of all personal data we hold.
2. **Right to Rectification:** Correct inaccurate personal data.
3. **Right to Erasure:** Request deletion of personal data ("right to be forgotten").
4. **Right to Portability:** Receive personal data in a structured, machine-readable format.

Requests are processed within 30 business days. We may request identity verification before fulfilling a data access request.

## Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that affects personal information, we will:

1. Notify affected users within 72 hours of discovery.
2. Report the breach to relevant regulatory authorities as required by law.
3. Provide a detailed incident report within 30 days, including root cause analysis and remediation steps.

## Compliance

This policy is compliant with GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), and SOC 2 requirements. Annual third-party audits verify compliance. Audit summaries are available upon request.

## Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Users will be notified of material changes via email at least 14 days before the changes take effect. Continued use of the service after notification constitutes acceptance.
