# Troubleshooting Guide

Common issues and their solutions. If your issue is not listed here, contact support through the Help Center.

## Login Issues

### "Invalid credentials" error

This error appears when the email or password is incorrect. Try the following:

1. Verify that Caps Lock is not enabled.
2. Ensure you are using the email address you registered with (check for typos).
3. If you recently changed your password, make sure you are using the new one.
4. Clear your browser cache and cookies, then try again.

If the issue persists, reset your password using the steps in the Account Management guide.

### Account locked after multiple failed attempts

For security, accounts are temporarily locked after 5 consecutive failed login attempts. The lockout duration is 15 minutes. Do not attempt to log in during this period — each failed attempt resets the timer.

If you believe your account has been compromised, contact security@example.com immediately.

## Performance Issues

### Slow page load times

If pages are loading slowly:

- Check your internet connection speed at https://speedtest.net.
- Disable browser extensions, which may interfere with page rendering.
- Try accessing the service from an incognito or private browsing window.
- If the issue affects all users, check our status page at https://status.example.com.

### File upload failures

File uploads may fail for the following reasons:

- **File too large:** Maximum upload size is 50 MB per file. Compress large files before uploading.
- **Unsupported format:** We accept PDF, DOCX, TXT, CSV, and PNG/JPG image files only.
- **Network timeout:** Uploads on slow connections may time out after 60 seconds. Use a stable connection.

## Notification Issues

### Not receiving email notifications

1. Check your spam or junk folder.
2. Add notifications@example.com to your email contacts or safe sender list.
3. Verify your notification preferences in Settings → Notifications.
4. If you use a corporate email, ask your IT administrator to whitelist our domain.

Email notifications may be delayed up to 5 minutes during peak usage periods. If you have not received a notification after 30 minutes, contact support.
