# Account Management

This guide covers common account operations including registration, password resets, and account closure.

## Creating an Account

To create a new account:

1. Navigate to the registration page at https://example.com/register.
2. Enter your full name, email address, and a password that meets our requirements.
3. Password requirements: minimum 12 characters, at least one uppercase letter, one number, and one special character.
4. Complete email verification by clicking the link sent to your email within 24 hours.

If you do not verify your email within 24 hours, you must restart the registration process.

## Resetting Your Password

If you forget your password:

1. Go to https://example.com/reset-password.
2. Enter the email address associated with your account.
3. Check your email for a reset link. The link expires after 1 hour.
4. Create a new password that meets the requirements above.

Note: Password reset links can only be used once. If the link has expired, request a new one. For security, we do not disclose whether an email address is registered when a reset is requested.

## Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

We strongly recommend enabling two-factor authentication:

- **TOTP Apps:** Use any TOTP-compatible app (e.g., Google Authenticator, Authy). Scan the QR code from your account settings page.
- **Recovery Codes:** When you enable 2FA, you receive 10 single-use recovery codes. Store these securely — they are the only way to regain access if you lose your authenticator device.
- **Disabling 2FA:** Requires entry of a valid TOTP code or a recovery code. Contact support if you have lost access to both.

## Closing Your Account

To permanently close your account:

1. Navigate to Settings → Account → Close Account.
2. Confirm by entering your password.
3. Data deletion begins immediately and completes within 30 days per our Data Privacy Policy.

Account closure is irreversible. We recommend exporting your data before closing your account (Settings → Data Export).
